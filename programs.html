Foundation — Class 7 & 8

Strong conceptual base and logical reasoning. This program builds the analytical skills students need for higher mathematics, focusing on number sense, geometry fundamentals, and algebraic thinking.


Key Focus Areas:

Arithmetic mastery, Introduction to algebra, Geometry foundations, Logical reasoning development

Madhyamik — Class 9 & 10

Structured preparation aligned with the WBBSE board. Students develop problem-solving strategies and exam techniques while building deep conceptual understanding of board-level mathematics.


Key Focus Areas:

Algebra & equations, Coordinate geometry, Trigonometry, Board exam preparation & practice

WBCHSE — Class 11 & 12

Semester-based rigorous mathematics training. Advanced topics are covered with depth and precision, preparing students for both board exams and competitive entrance examinations.


Key Focus Areas:

Calculus, Linear algebra, Probability & statistics, Semester exam mastery
